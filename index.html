About Me

I started my career in 2010 as a Mechanical Engineer within Ship Building and Naval Sector. I operated and maintained all kinds of electrical and mechanical machinery. Worked with oil tankers, frigates, destroyers. My passion, quest for more and more took me to submarines which I dove for 2 years and also looked after their mechanical equipment. Got a taste of coding onboard submarine when a few software engineer were working on one of the systems.

Got so much fascinated with it that when I moved to Melbourne in 2018, I decided to leave behind my mechanical engineering career and give it all to software engineering. Joined LeWagon's Full Stack Developer programme while working my day job as a Production Coordinator in a Manufacturing company. It was one of the best decisions of my life. After so many ups and downs, I am fully satisfied and happy with this path and coding truly makes me happy.
